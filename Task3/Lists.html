Definition List

Game of Thrones
A fantasy series based on George R.R. Martin's novels — full of noble houses, betrayal, and the fight for the Iron Throne.
House Stark
The noble family of the North, known for honor and loyalty. Their motto: “Winter is Coming.”
House Lannister
Rich and powerful family from the Westerlands, known for their cunning. Their motto: “Hear Me Roar!” (or unofficially “A Lannister always pays his debts.”)
